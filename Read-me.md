Agri-Rent: Farm Equipment Rental Platform
Overview

Agri-Rent is a location-based web platform designed to connect farmers with nearby agricultural equipment owners. The platform enables farmers to easily discover, book, and schedule farm machinery such as tractors, ploughs, rotavators, and more, reducing the cost burden of purchasing expensive equipment.

Problem Statement

Many farmers cannot afford costly agricultural machinery, while many equipment owners have idle machines that are not fully utilized. This project bridges that gap by creating a sharing economy for farm equipment.

Solution

Agri-Rent provides a digital marketplace where:

Farmers can search and rent equipment based on their location.
Equipment owners can list their machines for rent.
Both parties can connect easily and efficiently.

Features
> User registration and login system
> Separate dashboards for farmers and equipment owners
> Add, edit, and delete equipment listings
> Equipment categorization (Tractor, Plough, Rotavator, Harrow, Cultivator, Seed Drill, Planter, Transplanter, Dibber, Water Pump, etc.)
> Location-based search using map integration
> Booking and scheduling system
> Price and availability management
> Contact details management (name, mobile number, email)
> AI-based equipment suggestion system (future scope)
